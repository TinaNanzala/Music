POEM

FRIENDS

Friendship is one of lifes greatest treasures.
Friends that are loyal arte always there to
make you laugh when you are down,
They are not afraid to help you avoid mistakes
They never let you down nor ditch you
Always there to look out for your best interest.
This kind of friend can be hard to find,but
they offer a friendship that will last a lifetime.
That bracelet that is worn on your wrist meant for friendship
never gets off that wrist ,
Others may not be quite as loving
The pain caused by friendship marred by
Betrayal is not easy to overcome. Infact,
many find the in spiration from the joy brought brought about by a friend or the pain caused by a failed friendship.

Tina N.Nanzala
